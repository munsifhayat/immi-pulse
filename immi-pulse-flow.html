2. Pre-case lifecycle — Query → Pre-case → Case

The core pipeline a consultant operates on. A public questionnaire response or inbound email becomes a pre-case, gets AI-triaged, gets qualified, gets an engagement letter, gets paid, and converts into an active case. Every automated step has a manual override (skip payment, mark letter signed, force-convert) per the locked Phase 1 design.

stateDiagram-v2 [*] --> pending : public form submitted
or email arrived pending --> in_review : consultant opens in_review --> qualified : qualify (manual or AI-suggested) in_review --> archived : not a fit qualified --> letter_sent : compose + send engagement letter letter_sent --> letter_signed : client signs (PIN portal) letter_sent --> letter_signed : manual mark-signed (override) letter_signed --> paid : record manual payment letter_signed --> paid : skip payment (override) paid --> converted : open case qualified --> converted : force-convert (override) converted --> [*] : becomes Case archived --> [*]

3. Public questionnaire → triage → consultant inbox (sequence)

The single most important user journey to get production-ready: an applicant submits a public form, the backend creates a pre-case, AI triage classifies it, and it lands in the consultant's inbox with a suggested visa subclass and confidence. This sequence covers every hop including the Bedrock call and DB writes.
