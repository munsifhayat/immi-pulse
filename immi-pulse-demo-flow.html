PreCase lifecycle — the seven states a lead passes through

Every lead in IMMI-PULSE has exactly one of these statuses, and the sidebar tabs in your console map directly to them. You can manually override any transition (skip payment, mark a letter signed in person, archive at any moment) — that's a deliberate design choice for your pilot.

Document intelligence — what happens the moment a file is uploaded

Every document the applicant uploads is classified, flagged, and posted to the case timeline within seconds. The heuristic layer runs first so the portal feels instant; if Bedrock is reachable, deeper analysis overwrites the heuristic with a stronger result. Either way, you see flags, not a blank file icon.

Five things to leave the consultant with

  • Branded intake from day one. Their firm's name on the questionnaire URL — applicants never see ours.
  • AI assists, you decide. Triage and document flags are suggestions; every status change is a click you make.
  • Manual override at every step. Skip payment, mark a letter signed in person, archive at any time — the system never blocks you.
  • Multi-tenant isolation. Each consultancy is its own org, with its own seats, niche, template, bank details, and data.
  • Resilient by design. Heuristic fallbacks mean the demo works even if AWS Bedrock has a bad day.
