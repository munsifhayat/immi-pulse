How people use it

Three things, in under a minute — no friction, no form-walls.

1

Check their wait

Pick a visa and lodgement date. Instantly see where they sit on a real percentile band — and a plain-English “you’re tracking normally” verdict.

2

Compare the numbers

Toggle Official (Home Affairs figures) vs Community (what applicants actually report) for every visa, side by side.

3

Share their timeline

Add their own lodge → decision dates anonymously. Every submission makes the “is my wait normal?” answer better for the next person.

Honest by design. We show percentile bands (not a single misleading average), always display how many are still waiting, and label community data as a crowd-sourced sanity check — with a nudge to corroborate with an OMARA-registered agent.
“Find an Agent” is hidden for now. The agent directory is temporarily turned off so the public experience stays focused on the community + journey. Anyone visiting /find-consultants is gently redirected to the community. It’s one config line to switch back on when we’re ready.
This is the wedge. “Is my wait normal?” is the #1 reason applicants gather online. It’s the free front door — the data and trust it builds feed everything that comes next (personal timelines, cohorts, the agent directory, the consultant SaaS).
