Why this matters

A real week, lost — because the first hello had nowhere to land

This is the story that started it all, from one of our pilot consultants. It’s the whole product in one anecdote.

“

“He messaged me on WhatsApp — +91, so my brain said ‘probably a general inquiry.’ Our timing never matched. ‘I’ll call you Saturday at 9’… missed it… ‘sorry, Wednesday?’ This went on for a week. Turns out he was a 482 holder in Brisbane, an electrical engineer, who wanted to bring his wife over. A real, high‑value client — and we almost lost him to a scheduling mismatch.”

— Gideon, registered migration agent & pilot partner

The fix isn’t more chasing. It’s structure.

One link captures the right questions up front — which country are you in, what visa, what’s your situation — so the valuable cases surface themselves. From that first answer, every step has a clear home, and the client is brought into the same room as the consultant.

The shift: stop managing leads in your head and across five apps. Give every inquiry one path, and give every client one door.

Step by step

What each step looks like — expand any one

This is the new pre-case detail screen. Each step tells you what just happened, what it unlocks next, and always offers a manual way through. Click to open them.

1

A new inquiry lands — read for you

Becomes a Query in your inbox, already summarised
Automatic
What happens

Someone completes your form. We capture their visa interest and situation, and write a one‑paragraph summary with a suggested fit — so you triage in seconds, not minutes.

Unlocks next

You decide: qualify it into a pre-case, or set it aside. Nothing is committed yet.

Why it’s a “Query” first: you may get thousands of these. A Query is just an unactioned inbox item — no pressure, no clutter in your real pipeline.
2

Qualify it → now it’s a Pre-case

Your call: this one’s worth pursuing
Needs you
What happens

One tap marks the lead qualified. It leaves the noisy inbox and enters your real working pipeline as a pre‑case — a genuine prospect you intend to convert.

Unlocks next

The engagement letter — your costs agreement — becomes the clear next action.

Manual

Already spoke to them and want to skip straight ahead? Open a case directly from the client’s profile — no need to walk every step.

Clears up the old confusion: Query = haven’t actioned it. Pre-case = you’ve said yes, but nothing’s signed or paid yet.
3

Send the engagement letter

Scope, fees and disbursements — the compliance gate
Needs you
What happens

You compose the costs agreement — visa subclass, scope, professional fee, disbursements, retainer — from your saved template. It emails to the client with a secure signing link.

Unlocks next

The client signs — online, or you mark it signed on their behalf.

Manual

Prefer your own letterhead? Send your standard letter offline and record it here. The step still completes — the system never forces its template on you.

This is the legal line. Under the Code of Conduct you can’t start paid work without a written services-and-fees agreement — so this step protects you, not just the client.
4

The client signs

Type, draw, or you attest on their behalf
Skippable
What happens

The client opens the link, enters a one‑time PIN, and signs — by typing their name or drawing it. We keep a tamper‑proof record of exactly what they agreed to and when.

Unlocks next

Take the deposit — or move on if no money changes hands.

Manual

Signed in your office, on paper, or over a video call? Mark signed manually — right on this step — with a note of how it happened. Upload the scan if you have one.

Every signature method — online or manual — is logged for your file. That record is exactly what keeps you compliant.
5

Record the deposit

Bank transfer, PayID, cash — or waive it
Skippable
What happens

Log the retainer you received — method, amount, reference, date. A receipt number is generated for the file. No card processing required; you stay in control of the money.

Unlocks next

The big one — open the case and switch on the client’s portal.

Manual

Family member or pro‑bono? Skip payment with a reason. It records a clean waiver for your audit trail — the path doesn’t break.

Manual payments first, by design. We start where you already are — you mark money as received. Automated collection can come later.
6

Open the case — and the portal goes live

This is the moment the client joins you inside the platform
The unlock
What happens

The pre-case becomes a real case. We generate the document checklist for the chosen visa, create the client’s account, and email them a temporary PIN to log in — all automatically.

Unlocks next

Both of you now work the case in one shared space — documents, progress and messages.

Manual

Need to jump the queue? Force convert with a reason skips the remaining gates — useful for offline or high‑touch deals. Everything is still logged.

Know the visa first

Ask the visa question up front — then the right checklist builds itself

Today the visa is buried in free text and guessed at later. Instead, we capture it as a real choice on the intake form. That single answer decides which documents to ask for — and you stay in charge of those lists. Pick a category to see a sample.

The category your client picks

Skilled — points-tested

Independent and state-nominated skilled migration, driven by the points test and a skills assessment.

These are starting points — you edit them. Add, remove or rename items, and set which document types you’ll accept for each. Build a checklist per visa type that matches how you practise.
Documents requested

    Plus the cross‑cutting basics on every file — identity, Form 80, agent appointment (Form 956), health (HAP ID) and police clearances.
