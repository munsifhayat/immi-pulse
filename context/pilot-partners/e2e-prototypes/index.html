Three philosophies for the same work

The real question for this meeting: how much do you want to drive, how much do you want handed to you to approve, and how much should your client do alongside you?

Control
Approach A

Atelier Pipeline

You're the pilot · structured & auditable

A calm, editorial command center. An explicit, gated pipeline you move each case through by hand. The AI helps quietly from the margins — it never takes the wheel. Everything deterministic, everything on the record.

  • Left-rail pipeline; a guided vertical timeline per case
  • AI is a quiet assistant: summaries, suggested checklist, flags
  • You open each form, see it pre-filled, edit, mark verified
  • Closest to how the current product already works
Launch Approach A
Best for agents who want full control & an audit trail
Automation
Approach B

Pulse Copilot

It drafts · you approve

An AI clerk that never sleeps. It triages, summarises, recommends the visa, drafts the letter, builds the checklist, reads the documents and fills the forms — then hands each finished piece to you to Approve, Edit or Decline. It drafts everything; it sends and lodges nothing without you.

  • An approval queue + a live copilot chat dock
  • Confidence scores and source tags on every draft
  • "I filled 47 of 52 fields — review?" → one click
  • Human-in-the-loop by design (drafts, never sends)
Launch Approach B
Best for agents who want to supervise, not type
Collaboration
Approach C

Shared Case Room

You & your client, same room

One shared workspace. Your client answers friendly, plain-language questions and uploads a passport once — and the government forms assemble themselves on your side. Flip between the client's view and your view of the very same case. They never see "Form 80"; you receive a finished, verifiable one.

  • A live Client-view / Agent-view toggle of one case
  • "Collect once" — a shared information ledger
  • Client answers human questions; Form 80 fills live
  • Warm client portal + serious agent console, side by side

Best for agents who want a premium client experience
