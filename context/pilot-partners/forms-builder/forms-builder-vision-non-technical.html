Advanced · Logic Builder

Build complex flows visually.

The full canvas. Drag any field. Set any rule. Show, hide, skip, require, calculate, route, alert. The hard part of building intelligent forms — made as simple as dragging blocks.

  • Show / Hide fields based on prior answers
  • Skip entire sections that don't apply
  • Required-if rules (e.g. only ask employer if visa = 482)
  • Calculate (age from DOB, visa expiry countdown)
  • Flag & route (unlawful = urgent + senior partner)
Best for: practitioners with a real intake process they want the software to mirror exactly.

Before & After

Same inquiry. Different week.

The +91 WhatsApp ping again — but this time, with smart forms in place.

Before — the lost week

What happened on call 3

  • Inquiry arrives with zero context. Brain auto-assumes "low priority overseas inquiry".
  • A week of WhatsApp ping-pong. Time zones never align. Calls scheduled, missed, rescheduled.
  • Real situation only surfaces a week later — 482 holder, Brisbane, ready to be sponsored.
  • Could easily have been lost to a faster competitor.
  • No structured data. Nothing to triage. Nothing to summarise.

After — the smart intake

With logic-based forms

  • You reply with one link. Client fills it in 2 minutes from their phone.
  • Form asks the right next question based on every answer — "Australia" branches into visa status; "482" branches into occupation + employer.
  • Submission lands in your Inbox with a one-line AI summary: "482 holder, Brisbane, electrical engineer, employer ready to sponsor — qualified, urgent."
  • Auto-routed straight to qualified pre-case. Skip triage entirely.
  • Time from inquiry to engaged client: under an hour.
