# Visa Tracker Apps & Tools — Competitive Landscape

**Date:** April 2026

---

## Australia

### Official Government Tools

**ImmiAccount Real-Time Portal (NEW — March 2026)**
- Major overhaul launched March 6, 2026
- Live queue position — applicants see exactly where their file sits
- Push notifications when a case officer opens a file
- Color-coded dashboards (airline-style boarding queue metaphor)
- AI-driven triage layer for document completeness checks
- Standardized processing benchmarks: 6 months for 186 visa, 10 weeks for 482, 8 weeks for student visas
- "Specialist Skills" fast lane: 7 business day turnaround for salaries > AU$180,000
- Next phase (July 2026): Extension to partner and parent visas with myGov push notifications
- Built on cloud platform co-designed with Services Australia; expected to save AUD 12M annually

**myVEVO App (Department of Home Affairs)**
- Checks visa conditions: work rights, study rights, travel conditions, expiry date
- Can email visa details to employers/schools
- Google Play: 4.4/5 stars, 3,770 reviews, ~6,300 monthly downloads
- Free, iOS and Android
- Common complaints: persistent connectivity issues, frequent login failures, cumbersome multi-reference-number login

**Australian Immi App**
- Available in 47 countries as of March 2026
- Passport details and facial biometrics submission from smartphone
- Eliminates need for in-person biometric collection
- NOT a processing time tracker — biometric submission tool only

### Third-Party Trackers (Australia)

**MyImmiTracker (myimmitracker.com)**
- Dedicated Australian visa timeline tracker covering 186, 189, 190, 482, 500, and other subclasses
- Part of broader ImmiTracker platform (see below)

**australia-visa-timelines.com**
- Crowdsourced tracker specifically for Australian visa timelines
- Users compare their timeline against other applicants worldwide

## Canada

### Official Government Tools

**GCKey / IRCC Secure Account**
- Official portal for submitting applications, uploading documents, receiving officer messages
- Provides case status updates

**IRCC Application Status Tracker**
- Newer tool with more detailed updates for Express Entry, PR, family sponsorship, citizenship
- Community reports indicate it often updates faster than GCKey

### Third-Party Trackers (Canada)

**ImmiTrack (immitrack.ca) — iOS & Android**
- Countdown timer synced with official IRCC processing times
- Express Entry draw results with predictive draw number estimates
- Step-by-step immigration guides
- Task manager for application milestones
- Freemium model
- Covers: student visa, family sponsorship, skilled migration, visitor visa

**Canadian Immigration Tracker (by Thomas Garlick) — iOS & Android**
- No account required
- Official IRCC wait times for all application types
- Personalized completion estimates
- Historical trends and countdowns
- Immigration news updates

## United States

### Official Government Tools

**CEAC Status Tracker (ceac.state.gov)**
- US Department of State tool for nonimmigrant and immigrant visa applications
- Requires: application location, case number, passport number, surname
- No predictive features, just current status

**USCIS Case Status Online (egov.uscis.gov)**
- Tracks cases using receipt numbers
- Basic status updates only

### Third-Party Trackers (US)

**Case Tracker for USCIS & NVC (by ImmiVision, LLC)**
- 2+ million users
- 4.8/5 stars, 311,000+ ratings on iOS
- Available since October 2014 (longest-running)
- Tracks all valid USCIS receipt types + NVC cases
- Push notifications for status changes
- Civics quiz, visa bulletin, immigration news
- Revenue: Free with in-app purchases ($2.99 - $18.99)
- Complaints: Increased ad frequency, "fake push notifications" misleading users

**Lawfully (lawfully.com)**
- 2.5+ million users tracking 5+ million cases
- 4.8/5 stars, 157,000+ ratings on iOS
- Covers USCIS, NVC, and EOIR cases
- AI-powered predictions and processing time analysis
- Mock interview prep with former consular officers
- Lawyer directory and video consultations
- Community: 200K+ posts, 3M+ comments, 6M+ registered case statuses
- Revenue: Subscription-based ($9.99 - $154.99)
- Funding: $2.5M raised (Blue Point Capital, TBT, Lotte Ventures)
- Founded 2017, NYC

**MigraConnect Case Tracker (by Tecso Solutions LLC)**
- iOS: 4.88/5 stars, 130,000+ ratings
- Android: 4.90/5 stars, 40,000+ ratings, ~13,000 monthly downloads
- Only app combining USCIS + FOIA + Immigration Court updates
- Asylum Clock tracking
- AI predictions (free for everyone)
- Full case history (past updates not shown on USCIS website)
- Multi-language (English/Spanish)
- Ad-free, privacy-focused
- Revenue: Free, community supported

**Track My Visa (trackmyvisanow.com)**
- 25,000+ users
- 4.9/5 Trustpilot rating
- Tracks I-129F (fiance) and I-130 (family-based) only
- Data-driven forecasts using USCIS developer API
- Probability calculations for next status change
- Position-in-line tracking
- Email alerts (free: 1-2x/day; PRO: hourly)
- Auto-cancels subscription when case reaches NVC
- Discord community

**VisaGrader (visagrader.com)**
- Web-only platform
- Covers: H1B tracker, I-485 tracker, 221(g) tracker, dropbox/interview waiver tracker
- Visa bulletin with predictions
- Wait times and slot availability by country
- Revenue: Free (ad-supported)

**Trackitt (trackitt.com)**
- One of the oldest immigration trackers
- Employment-based green card focus (PERM, I-140, I-485, EAD)
- Green card prediction tool using historical data
- 2/5 stars on some platforms — appears dated

**VisaWatch (visawatch.app)**
- Newer entrant
- 24/7 USCIS tracking with instant push notifications
- Translates cryptic USCIS messages into plain language with next steps
- Ad-free, no referral schemes

**ImmiHelp (immihelp.com)**
- Long-running community platform with crowd-sourced trackers
- Completely volunteer-driven data, free

**VisaJourney (visajourney.com)**
- 20+ years old, one of the oldest immigration communities
- Ranked #13 in Immigration & Visas category globally
- Timeline tracker with estimated approval dates
- Strong forum community, free

## Cross-Platform (Multi-Country)

**MyImmiTracker (myimmitracker.com / immitracker.app)**
- 200,000+ registered users, 9,000+ community members
- 20+ programs tracked across Canada & Australia
- Trustpilot: 4.9/5 stars (77 reviews, 95% five-star)
- Described as "the Waze of Immigration"
- Headquarters: Toronto, Canada
- Key features: Case-by-case tracking, analytics, comparison tools, service marketplace, community forums
- Revenue — Freemium:
  - Free: Full access to trackers, case filtering/sorting, comparison, community
  - $5/month: Analytics reports, predictive reporting, private messaging
  - $20 lifetime: All premium + 10% GCMS notes discount + free 15-min consultation
  - Marketplace commissions (GCMS notes at $18, consultations)
- Likes: Most comprehensive crowdsourced data, excellent analytics, strong community
- Dislikes: Data relies on voluntary submissions, limited to CA and AU only (no US)
