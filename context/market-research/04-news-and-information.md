## Processing Time Estimation — How Tools Work

### Three Approaches

**A. Government-Published Data (backward-looking)**
- USCIS: 80th percentile of completed cases
- IRCC: 80th percentile, weekly (temp) or monthly (permanent)
- Australia DHA: 75th percentile, monthly
- Reasonably reliable for aggregate trends; individual cases vary significantly

**B. AI/ML Prediction Models**
- Lawfully, VisaIQ analyze historical data across nationality, form type, service center, season, backlog
- Train on millions of cases to predict individual timelines
- Accuracy: 20-50% variance depending on circumstances
- Limitation: Sudden policy changes disrupt model accuracy

**C. Crowdsourced/Community Data**
- ImmiTracker, Trackitt, VisaJourney aggregate self-reported milestones
- Subject to sample bias (people with problems/recent decisions more likely to report)
- Strength: Often surfaces trends before official data reflects them

### Key Methodological Gap
Government data uses different percentiles (USCIS: 80th, Australia: 75th, IRCC: 80th), different frequencies, and different calculation methods. **No platform normalizes this for cross-country comparison.**
