**Date:** April 2026

## Established Case Management Platforms (US-focused)

### Docketwise
- **What:** All-in-one immigration case management — forms auto-fill, case tracking, client portal, CRM, e-filing, billing
- **Target:** Small to mid-size US immigration law firms
- **Pricing:** $69/user/mo (Basic), $89/user/mo (Pro), $109/user/mo (Advanced)
- **Key features:** 100% immigration-focused; USCIS form auto-fill; multilingual questionnaires (8 languages); e-filing with USCIS; client portal; priority date tracking
- **Technology:** Traditional SaaS with some automation; not AI-native
- **Market position:** Market leader for SMB immigration firms
- **Limitations:** Invoicing inadequate (manual per-client); slow to add new USCIS forms; Clio integration unreliable; no non-US immigration support

### INSZoom (by Mitratech)
- **What:** Enterprise-grade immigration case management and compliance
- **Target:** Large law firms, corporate immigration teams, hospitals, universities
- **Pricing:** Starts ~$50/mo, enterprise pricing requires sales contact
- **Key features:** Forms for 80+ countries updated within 24hrs of release; compliance tracking; configurable workflows; custom reporting; e-filing
- **Technology:** Traditional cloud SaaS, legacy architecture
- **Market position:** Established (20+ years). Enterprise readiness: 9.0/10, Scalability: 8.8/10
- **Limitations:** Dated, clunky UI; poor Mac support; slow customer support; steep learning curve

### Envoy Global
- **What:** Full-service global immigration management — legal representation + technology
- **Target:** Enterprise/Fortune 500 with global mobility needs
- **Pricing:** Platform is free; revenue from legal service fees
- **Key features:** Employee comms; case status tracking; HRIS/HCM integration (Workday, ADP); thousands of automated workflows across countries; VIP prioritization
- **Funding:** $21M raised (2017)
- **Limitations:** Overkill for SMBs; opaque pricing; not standalone software

### LollyLaw
- **What:** All-in-one immigration practice management
- **Target:** US immigration attorneys, small to mid-size firms
- **Pricing:** ~$120/user/mo
- **Key features:** 125+ USCIS/DOJ/EOIR/FBI forms; 20+ language translations; real-time collaboration; QR code/SMS payments; customizable case workflows
- **Limitations:** No AI; US only; higher pricing

### eImmigration (by Cerenade)
- **What:** Case management for immigration law practices and non-profits
- **Target:** US immigration firms and non-profits
- **Pricing:** $55 - $85/user/mo; 50% non-profit discount; no per-case fees
- **Key features:** 300+ immigration forms; auto-fill + e-filing; custom fields/reports/automations; billing/scheduling
- **Limitations:** Confusing Petitioner/Alien conflicts; cannot manage non-immigration cases alongside

### CampLegal
- **What:** Immigration case management with dedicated client app
- **Target:** US immigration law firms
- **Pricing:** $69/user/mo (Essentials); Pro tier with AI
- **Key features:** USCIS form automation with bi-directional sync; client mobile app; 18 languages; conditional logic questionnaires; LawPay + QuickBooks integrations; visa bulletin monitoring

### FastVisa
- **What:** Immigration workflow automation
- **Target:** Small to mid-size US practices
- **Pricing:** $45/user/mo (lowest in market)
- **Key features:** Customizable intake; form auto-populate; N/A auto-fill for USCIS compliance; client portal; messaging; unlimited document storage

### Filevine ImmigrationAI
- **What:** AI-powered immigration module on Filevine's legal platform
- **Target:** Immigration firms using/considering Filevine
- **Pricing:** Requires Filevine Docs+ subscription
- **Key features:** AI scans passports/transcripts/letters in 170 languages; auto-fills USCIS forms; real-time USCIS + NVC tracking; full practice management
- **Technology:** AI-powered (OCR extraction, document intelligence)
- **Limitations:** Requires full Filevine ecosystem buy-in

## Australian-Specific Immigration Software

### Migration Manager ⭐ (Market Leader)
- **What:** Market-leading case management built specifically for Australian immigration
- **Target:** Australian RMAs (Registered Migration Agents) and immigration lawyers — sole practitioners to Big 4 firms
- **Users:** 2,000+ lawyers and agents
- **Pricing:** Not public; demo required
- **Key features:**
  - NSW Law Society certified trust accounting
  - ImmiAccount integration (eLodge automation)
  - Batch VEVO search
  - Client portal (MM Portal)
  - Questionnaire tool
  - Desktop app for offline work
  - Sponsor obligations tracking
  - Humanitarian visa handling
- **Technology:** Traditional, desktop + web hybrid; ISO 27001:2022 certified
- **Market position:** Dominant in Australia. Partners with Law Council, Migration Institute, major universities
- **Critical limitation:** Legacy architecture (desktop dependency); NO AI capabilities; no modern API integrations

### Officio (Australia)
- **What:** Practice management suite for Australian migration professionals
- **Key features:** Populates ImmiAccount forms from questionnaire data; VEVO and ABN/ACN verification; multilingual questionnaires; client portal; milestone billing
- **Limitations:** No AI; limited integration ecosystem

### Ezymigrate
- **What:** CRM and case management for immigration consultants
- **Origin:** New Zealand, expanding to AU, CA, USA, Europe
- **Users:** 500+ immigration advisors (primarily NZ)
- **Key features:** Case workflows; instant visa form completion; client portal; SMS/email; Xero integration; agent portal; mobile app
- **Technology:** Cloud-based on Microsoft Azure
- **Limitations:** NZ-centric; AU support may be less mature

### EngageU (Australia)
- **What:** CRM for visa and immigration consultancy
- **Focus:** Workflow automation, skilled migration tracking
- **Market position:** Niche Australian player

### Smokeball (Australia)
- **What:** General legal practice management with immigration specialization
- **Technology:** Modern cloud-based
- **Limitation:** Immigration is one of many practice areas — not specialist

## AI-Powered Immigration Startups

### Parley (YC S24) 🔥
- **What:** AI that automates reading, writing, and compiling visa applications — 80% of the work
- **Target:** US immigration lawyers (employment-based visas)
- **Funding:** YC S24 batch
- **Key features:** O-1A Writer with dynamic evidence pulling; works IN Microsoft Word; supports O-1A, EB-1, EB-2/NIW, H-1B, TN, L-1, E-2; USCIS case status API
- **Notable:** Meets lawyers where they work (Word), not forcing new platform
- **Limitation:** US only

### Visalaw.ai
- **What:** AI legal research, drafting, and document analysis
- **Target:** Immigration attorneys and paralegals
- **Funding:** $1.6M seed (Valor Ventures)
- **Pricing:** $220/user/mo (Standard); $380/user/mo (Pro — 10 petitions/mo); Enterprise
- **Key features:** Petition drafting; legal research with AILA citations; document Q&A; PrivateGPT; exhibit assembly; immigration law library
- **Technology:** AI-native; SOC 2 Type II; no data retention
- **Notable:** Co-founded by prominent immigration lawyer Greg Siskind
- **Limitation:** US only

### DraftyAI
- **What:** AI drafting tool for immigration legal documents
- **Target:** Immigration attorneys
- **Pricing:** $79/mo (cheapest AI tool in market)
- **Key features:** Drafts petitions, RFEs, motions, asylum briefs, waivers, recommendation letters; circuit-aware citations; smart upload auto-fills
- **Notable:** Does one thing well at lowest price point
- **Limitation:** Drafting only, not case management; US only

### Gale (YC W25)
- **What:** Automates entire H-1B application and compliance
- **Target:** US employers sponsoring H-1B workers
- **Funding:** $2.7M seed (Axiom Partners, Pioneer Fund, 468 Capital, Elevation Capital, YC)
- **Key features:** Resume/passport upload auto-processing; HRIS integrations; H-1B, L-1, green card in one dashboard; licensed attorney review
- **Notable:** Founded by immigrants; expanding to O-1, F, K, TN visas
- **Limitation:** US employment visas only

### LegalOS (YC W26)
- **What:** "AI-native immigration law firm" — AI agents draft petitions, compile evidence, anticipate USCIS objections
- **Target:** Companies and individuals (employment-based US visas)
- **Funding:** $500K seed; YC W26
- **Key features:** Studied 12,000 successful petitions; 48-hour turnaround; 100% approval rate so far; O-1, H-1B, L-1A/B, TN, EB-1A/C, EB-2 NIW
- **Notable:** Hybrid law firm + tech; Forbes 30 Under 30 founder
- **Limitation:** US only; operates as law firm, not pure SaaS

### Imagility
- **What:** End-to-end AI immigration platform
- **Target:** US immigration attorneys, corporate HR, petitioners
- **Key features:** OCR-based data extraction; petition analysis (compliance, missing details); auto-filled forms; AI flags inconsistencies; H-1B, Green Card, I-140, L, E, O, TN
- **Notable:** Well-reviewed on G2

### OpenSphere
- **What:** AI-powered visa assessment for startup founders
- **Target:** Founders seeking O-1A, EB-1A, EB-2 NIW
- **Key features:** AI scoring against visa criteria; 15-day filing; founder-specific strategy mapping; recommendation letter targeting
- **Limitation:** Niche (founders only)

### SkipLegal
- **What:** Consumer-facing AI immigration assistant (chatbot)
- **Target:** Individual immigrants (B2C)
- **Pricing:** Tiered (10/50/unlimited docs per month)
- **Key features:** 24/7 AI assistant; guided form completion; document processing; real-time error checking; auto-translation; 95%+ accuracy
- **Technology:** AES-256 encryption; SOC 2 compliant
- **Launched:** April 2025, Boston

### Visto AI (Canada) 🇨🇦
- **What:** Automates Canadian immigration applications (80% faster)
- **Target:** Canadian RCICs
- **Key features:** AI chatbot trained on IRCC data; auto-generated checklists; Chrome extension for Express Entry/PR Portal autofill; auto-merge/rename documents; cover letter generation
- **Notable:** Pioneer in Canadian immigration tech

### VisaFlo (Canada) 🇨🇦
- **What:** AI-powered Canadian immigration software
- **Target:** Canadian RCICs and immigration lawyers
- **Pricing:** $129/mo (starts); free lightweight CRM tier
- **Key features:** AI data extraction; IRCC form and portal autofill (IMM PDFs + online portals); Online Representative Portal autofill; client intake automation
- **Status:** Invitation-only access currently

### ContentSnare (Australian company)
- Document and content collection with automated reminders
- ISO 27001; clean interface
- Used by immigration consultants among other industries

### CaseTracker.io
- USCIS case status tracking and client notifications
- 500+ law firms
- Bulk receipt import; WhatsApp/email notifications
- Integrates alongside Docketwise/INSZoom
- Simple per-firm pricing (no per-user fees)

## Key Takeaway for IMMI-PULSE

**The Australian B2B market has essentially ONE legacy option (Migration Manager) and ZERO AI-powered alternatives.** Every AI startup in immigration tech is focused on the US market. This represents a massive first-mover opportunity for an AI-native platform serving ~7,000 OMARA-registered agents in Australia.
